RADIUS RECORDS

About Us

Founded in September, we have come a long way with many great artists who have had thousands of streams. Our fast growing fan base has supported us and helped us to give our time and support to the artists who deserve to be heard. As time progress we have big plans for the next few months so follow us on social media to be kept updated. Look out for the Radius Virtual Festival, this summer, where some of our best artist will be playing. Tickets will be on sale soon.

Our Top Artists

PYRO

PYRO is our most successful artists with songs getting thousands of streams and his most recent album, 2002, going Radius Platinum in the first week of release. He is a big part of the label and not only provides good music but is a good supporter of the label. We hope to continue to work with him as he grows.

Jelver

Jelver has been with us for a while and is another great musician. His talent is clearly reflected in his amazing tunes which will get you on your feet. He has helped us grow as a label and continues to be a great part of Radius Records. We know this is the beginning of something big.

Hevlo

A recent signing of ours is Hevlo, a dedicated and talented musician who helps the community with his informative tutorials on music software, FL Studio. He is one of best artists with his recent tracks being very successful. We hope to help him get the numbers he truly deserves. Hevlo is one of out top artists.
